Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Our Commitment: TrueVault VPN operates under a strict zero-logs policy. We do not track, collect, or store your browsing activity, connection logs, IP addresses, or DNS queries. Your privacy is our top priority.

1. Information We Collect

TrueVault VPN collects minimal information necessary to provide our service and process payments. Here's exactly what we collect:

Account Information (Required)

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, login, password recovery, and service notifications
  • Password: Securely hashed and salted, never stored in plain text
  • Account Creation Date: For billing and subscription management
  • Subscription Plan: Personal, Family, or Dedicated Server

Payment Information (Via PayPal)

  • Payment Transaction IDs: For billing records and refund processing
  • Subscription Status: Active, cancelled, or expired
  • Billing Dates: Next payment due date

Note: We do not store credit card numbers, bank details, or any payment credentials. All payment processing is handled securely by PayPal.

Optional Settings (User-Configured)

  • Port Forwarding Rules: If you set up port forwarding, we store your device IPs and port mappings
  • Parental Control Settings: If enabled, we store calendar schedules and device restrictions
  • Device Names: Optional custom names you assign to your devices

3. How We Use Your Information

The minimal information we collect is used solely for:

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your VPN account
  • Billing & Payments: Processing subscriptions and handling refunds
  • Customer Support: Responding to your questions and troubleshooting issues
  • Service Delivery: Providing VPN access and optional features (port forwarding, parental controls)
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and abuse of our service
  • Communications: Sending service-related emails (subscription confirmations, payment receipts, security alerts)

We do NOT use your information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or data mining.

4. Information Sharing

TrueVault VPN does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in these limited circumstances:

Payment Processing

We use PayPal for payment processing. When you subscribe, PayPal receives your payment information (credit card, billing address) but TrueVault does not store or have access to this data. PayPal's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment information.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request. However, due to our zero-logs policy, we have no browsing history or connection logs to provide.

Business Transfers

If TrueVault VPN is acquired or merged with another company, your account information may be transferred to the new owner. You will be notified in advance and given the option to delete your account.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with individual salts
  • Database Security: Access is restricted to essential personnel only
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security reviews and vulnerability testing
  • Server Security: Servers are located in secure data centers with physical security measures

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained for the duration of your subscription
  • Cancelled Accounts: Account data retained for 30 days in case you return, then permanently deleted
  • Billing Records: Payment transaction IDs retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Support Emails: Retained for 1 year after resolution for quality assurance
