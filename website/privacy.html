Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

TrueVault VPN is built on a simple principle: your privacy is absolute. We do not log, monitor, or sell your data. Period.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (hashed) for account creation
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through PayPal. We never see or store your credit card numbers
  • Device Information: Public keys for WireGuard connections (required for VPN functionality)

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We have a strict no-logs policy. We do not collect:

  • Your browsing history
  • Traffic destinations
  • Data content
  • DNS queries
  • Connection timestamps
  • Session duration
  • Your originating IP address
  • Any information that could identify what you do online

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing subscription payments
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending essential service communications
  • Improving our service (aggregated, anonymous data only)

4. Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Encrypted database storage

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • PayPal: For payment processing. Subject to PayPal's Privacy Policy
  • Server Providers: For VPN infrastructure. No user data is shared or logged

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information only as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Payment records are retained for legal/tax purposes (7 years)
  • Support tickets are anonymized after 1 year

8. Children's Privacy

TrueVault VPN is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our service.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@vpn.the-truth-publishing.com
  • Support: Through your dashboard
