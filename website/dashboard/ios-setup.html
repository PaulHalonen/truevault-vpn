Get TrueVault VPN running on your iPhone or iPad

1

Install WireGuard App

Download the official WireGuard app from the App Store:

üì• Get WireGuard on App Store
2

Get Your VPN Configuration

  1. Open your dashboard on any device
  2. Click "+ Add Device"
  3. Name it (e.g., "My iPhone")
  4. Choose your server location
  5. Click "Generate Config"
3

Import Configuration

Using QR Code (Recommended):

  1. Open WireGuard app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap "Add a tunnel" or the + button
  3. Select "Create from QR code"
  4. Point camera at QR code on your screen
  5. Name it "TrueVault" and tap "Save"
4

Connect

  1. Toggle the switch next to your tunnel
  2. Allow VPN configuration when prompted
  3. You'll see "VPN" in your status bar
