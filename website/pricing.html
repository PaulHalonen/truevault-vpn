Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?
All features of your chosen plan are fully accessible during the 7-day trial. No credit card required to start. If you love it, upgrade to a paid plan. If not, no charges ever.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely! Cancel from your dashboard with one click. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you keep logs?
Never. We have a strict zero-logs policy. We don't track, collect, or store your browsing activity, connection times, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal through our secure payment processor.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference for fairness.
What's the difference between plans?
Personal (3 devices, basic features), Family (10 devices + parental controls + gaming controls), Dedicated (unlimited devices + your own private server + VIP support).
Is there a money-back guarantee?
The 7-day free trial serves as our guarantee. Try before you buy! If you're not satisfied after becoming a paid customer, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
How do I set up parental controls?
Parental controls are included in Family and Dedicated plans. After signing up, go to Dashboard > Parental Controls to set time limits, block categories, and schedule internet access with our visual calendar.
